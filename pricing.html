Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

Do you offer payment in installments?+

Yes! For projects over LKR 50,000, we offer flexible payment plans — typically 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. Contact us to discuss your preferred arrangement.

Can I get a custom quote for my project?+

Absolutely! Every business is unique. Contact us through our Contact page with your project details and we'll prepare a custom quote within 24 hours.

What is included in the support period?+

Support includes bug fixes, minor content updates, technical assistance, and guidance. Major feature additions after launch may be quoted separately.

Do prices include hosting and domain?+

Our development prices do not include hosting and domain costs. However, we can recommend the best affordable options and assist with the full setup process.

How long does a project typically take?+

Starter websites take 1–2 weeks, Business sites take 2–4 weeks, and E-commerce or System projects typically take 4–8 weeks depending on complexity.
