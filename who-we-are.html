Our Journey

Born From

DiSSMaC Global was founded by five friends who met during their IT undergraduate studies and discovered a shared passion for building digital solutions that make a real difference. What started as small projects between classmates quickly grew into a full-fledged IT company.

Today we offer web development, digital advertising, and custom business system creation — bringing fresh ideas and modern technical skills to every project we take on.
